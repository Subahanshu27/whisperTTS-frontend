Overview

Swapping faces in images has come a long way from manual cut-and-paste edits — the latest AI-powered tools now make it possible to achieve unbelievably realistic results, right from your desktop. With Qwen Image Edit 2509 and its precision inpainting system, anyone can replace faces or even entire heads in photographs, group shots, or creative compositions with just a few guided steps. This process is popular for memes, social media, and creative digital art, but it's also used in marketing, product shoots, and professional photo editing where maintaining a natural look and fine detail truly matters.

For best results:

  1. Upload images.
  2. In the first image mask only face and hair. The accuracy of the selection is important!
  3. In the reference image, mask only face and hair too. You can use big brush. The accuracy of the selection is NOT important. You do that for proper crop operation.
  4. Generate image.
  5. Play with color correction (Control color match strength).
